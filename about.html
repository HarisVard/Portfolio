About Me

Greetings! My name is Tony Natsios, a proficient 3D Character and Prop Artist based in Glasgow, Scotland. I specialize in using industry-leading software such as Autodesk Maya, ZBrush, and various Adobe Products. My passion lies in crafting realistic and captivating 3D characters, busts, and a wide range of assets and props, including hard surface models. As a diligent and dedicated professional, I pride myself on consistently delivering high-quality work before deadlines. My strong communication skills and patience, honed through my experience as a teacher, enable me to collaborate effectively with diverse teams, while also confidently tackling solo projects when required. Cheers!
