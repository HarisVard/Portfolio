About Me

Hello! My name is Tony Natsios and I am a 3D Character/Prop Artist and I live in Glasgow, Scotland. I am experienced in Autodesk Maya, ZBrush and Adobe Products. My focus as an artist is to create and deliver 3D characters, busts as long as 3D assets/props like hard surface models. Discipline and dedication are two of my strong characteristics making me a trustworthy person who can deliver a product before the deadline. Good communication skills and patience scales gained through my experience as teacher. I can work as part of a team but I have the confidence to work solo if that is necessary. Cheers!
